How it Works

It shouldn’t be complicated, so we made it easy.

Airtime is really simple. Put as much or as little cash into your kid’s airtime bank and choose how much text, talk, and data you want them to have, at any given time, for a price that makes sense. Change how the airtime can be used at any time. No hidden fees, no additional surcharges, and no complicated monthly plans.
